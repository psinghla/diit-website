About Us

A trusted computer institute in Hisar since 2001

DIIT was built to make government-recognised computer education affordable and genuinely well-taught for the families of Hisar — not to chase trends.

Who we are

Drishti Institute of Information Technology

DIIT is a NIELIT-accredited computer education and training centre in Hisar, run by a society that has been teaching since 2001. For over two decades we've helped students earn the certifications that open doors — government clerical posts, college admissions, accounting jobs and a first real footing in technology.

We deliberately stay a real, in-person institute. Classes happen in our classrooms and labs, with teachers you can ask questions to in the room — not a stream of videos you watch alone.

  • NIELIT-accredited — certificates with pan-India recognition
  • Fees set for local families, with installment options
  • In-person classes at our Hisar campus

What we stand for

How we teach

A few principles that have kept students coming back — and sending their siblings and friends — for over twenty years.

Recognised certifications

We focus on NIELIT programs and skills that carry weight on a real résumé and in government job eligibility.

Understand the why

Our programming track, led by an IIT Kanpur alumnus, teaches the reasoning behind the code — not just steps to memorise.

Affordable & accessible

Cost should never be the reason a student in Hisar can't start. Installments and honest pricing are part of the institute.

Faculty

Programming led by an IIT Kanpur alumnus

Our programming courses — Python, C, C++ and JavaScript — are led by Prashant Singhla, B.Tech, IIT Kanpur. The teaching philosophy is simple: understand why something works, and the how takes care of itself.

Alongside, our NIELIT and Tally programs are taught by experienced instructors who've guided thousands of Hisar students through certification and into jobs.
