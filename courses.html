FAQs

Common questions about our courses

Is the O Level course valid for government jobs?
Yes. O Level is a NIELIT/DOEACC-certified foundation IT diploma recognised across India and accepted as proof of computer proficiency for a wide range of government and public-sector roles.
What is the difference between CCC and O Level?
CCC (3 months) certifies basic computer concepts and is enough for many clerical government posts. O Level (1 year) is a deeper, diploma-level qualification covering IT fundamentals, office tools, web basics and programming logic — better if you want stronger eligibility and skills.
Are classes online or in-person?
All courses are taught in person at our Hisar campus, with hands-on lab practice and teachers you can ask questions to in the room.
Do you offer fee installments?
Yes. Fees are set for local families and most courses can be paid in easy installments. Contact us for the current fee and installment options for your course.
