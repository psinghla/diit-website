FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about DIIT, our courses and admissions in Hisar.

Is DIIT a NIELIT-accredited institute?
Yes. DIIT (Drishti Institute of Information Technology) is a NIELIT-accredited (formerly DOEACC) computer training centre in Hisar, run by a society educating since 2001. Our certifications carry pan-India recognition.
Which courses does DIIT offer?
Six NIELIT programs (O Level, CCC, CCC Plus, BCC, ACC, ECC), Tally Prime (Basic & Advanced), programming (Python, C, C++, JavaScript), and AI internship tracks including cyber security and digital marketing. See all courses.
Where is DIIT located in Hisar?
We are between Camp Chowk & Matka Chowk, behind IDBI Bank, Hisar, Haryana. Open in Google Maps.
What are the class timings?
The institute is open Monday to Saturday, 8 AM to 6 PM. Batch timings are flexible — contact us to find a slot that suits you.
Do you offer fee installments?
Yes. Fees are kept affordable for local families and most courses can be paid in easy installments. Enquire now for current fees.

Not sure which course is right for you?

Tell us your goal — government job, college, business or general skills — and we'll suggest the right course in 5 minutes.
